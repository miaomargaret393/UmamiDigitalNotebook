Leadership

  • Princeton Biotechnology Group

    Director (December 2023–June 2025), Design Lead (December 2022–December 2023), Advisor (Present)

    As part of the founding team, I aimed to create an inclusive, multidisciplinary space at Princeton comprising of both undergraduate and graduate students, where any student interested in biotech could get involved! I helped plan and led over five fully funded trips to NYC, Boston, and New Jersey (raising over $15k), ran a 500+ member community with biweekly newsletters, and hosted events with speakers from Schrödinger, Eli Lilly, BMS, startups, and venture capital. I established an online presence for the club through my marketing and design work over the PBG's website and LinkedIn. Through the club, many students were able to gain valuable connections, have conversations with Founders, PIs, recruiters, and find jobs/internships. Most importantly, we established a community. Live, Laugh, PBG!

  • IgniteSTEM

    Design Lead (September 2022–May 2024)

I developed branded slide decks, visual assets, and program materials that created a consistent identity across events and improved the participant experience, supporting our broader goal of making STEM learning more engaging and accessible for all students.

  • Princeton Pitch Competition
