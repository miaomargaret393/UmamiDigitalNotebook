art

These are the art pieces I've created for AP Art Drawing. My investigation is focused on the theme of "How can I use art to depict internal struggles and emotions?" I explore this theme through various mediums, including digital art, traditional drawing, and mixed media like using fire to see how heat dynamically changes the portriat. The pieces follow a main protagonit, whose internal struggles are projected into reality, depicting a different strife of the human experience.
